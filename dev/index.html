Kinetic.jl is a lightweight toolbox for modeling and simulation on the basis of kinetic theory of gases, photons, plasmas and neutrons. It can be used to solve either Boltzmann and related model equations

\[\frac{\partial f}{\partial t}+ \mathbf u \cdot \nabla_\mathbf x f + \mathbf a \cdot \nabla_\mathbf u f = \int_{\mathcal R^3} \int_{\mathcal S^2} B (f(\mathbf u_*')f(\mathbf u')-f(\mathbf u_*) f(\mathbf u)) d\Omega d\mathbf u_*\]

or their upscaling moment system

\[\frac{\partial \mathbf W}{\partial t} + \nabla_\mathbf x \cdot \mathbf F = \mathbf S\]

The package is registered in the Julialang official entry and compatible with Julia 1.3 or newer version. To make use of it, execute Julia and type

julia> ]
(v1.3) pkg> add Kinetic

This will install Kinetic and all its dependencies. After that, load the package,

julia> using Kinetic
